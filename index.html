About Me


I am a risk and analytics professional with experience across financial markets and data-driven reporting environments. My background spans market and credit risk alongside analytics implementation, where I focus on transforming complex datasets into structured, scalable insights that support informed decision-making.


Key Skills and Expertise

Risk & Financial Markets: I have experience with portfolio risk metrics including sensitivity analysis and stress testing across fixed income, FX and equity asset classes, including derivative products. My expertise includes ensuring accuracy and consistency of risk reporting through data validation and reconciliation processes.

Data Analysis & Reporting: Proficient in SQL, Python and Excel-based automation, I design and implement reporting workflows that improve data reliability and operational efficiency. I have built dashboards and structured reporting solutions using platforms such as Tableau and Power BI to enhance transparency and performance visibility.

Scalable Analytics Solutions: I focus on developing structured, repeatable analytics processes that strengthen reporting frameworks and support evolving business or regulatory requirements. My approach emphasises accuracy, consistency and usability in data delivery.

Stakeholder Engagement: I work closely with both business stakeholders and technical teams to translate data into practical insights. I am comfortable explaining technical findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders while maintaining analytical rigour.


Why Work With Me

Business-Centric Approach: I approach every project or task with a business-centric mindset, tailoring my analytical approach to meet the unique requirements and goals of each organization I work with.

Continuous Development: I remain committed to strengthening both technical and domain expertise, ensuring the solutions I deliver remain aligned with industry standards and evolving risk practices.
